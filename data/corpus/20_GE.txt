FAIRFIELD, Conn. (AP) _ General Electric Co. (GE) on Friday reported third-quarter earnings of $2.51 billion.

The Fairfield, Connecticut-based company said it had profit of 25 cents per share. Earnings, adjusted for non-recurring costs and to account for discontinued operations, came to 32 cents per share.

The results exceeded Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of 11 analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 26 cents per share.

The industrial conglomerate posted revenue of $31.68 billion in the period, also surpassing Street forecasts. Eleven analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $28.67 billion.

GE shares have climbed 11 percent since the beginning of the year, while the Standard & Poor's 500 index has dropped roughly 2 percent. The stock has increased 15 percent in the last 12 months.
