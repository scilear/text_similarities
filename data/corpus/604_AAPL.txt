NEW YORK (TheStreet) -- If the Muggles have got you down, think again, Harry Potter fans. Today is your lucky day.

Apple (AAPL - Get Report) teamed up with author J.K. Rowling to bring out new versions of the Harry Potter books which will be available exclusively on iBooks.

Previously digital versions of the books were available only via the Pottermore Shop -- Rowling's digital entertainment, news and e-commerce company. On iBooks, the seven books in the Harry Potter series will have the original text, enhanced by interactive animations as well as elaborate artwork to help bring the stories to life.

Here is a list of the books:

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (Published in the U.S. as Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone) Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

"Harry Potter fans are going to love how their favorite stories come to life," said Apple CEO Timothy D. Cook in a statement. "J.K. Rowling's legendary series is perfect for enjoying on your iPad or iPhone, and we're thrilled to offer them exclusively on the iBooks Store."

Rowling is currently working on a Harry Potter series of prequels entitled Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them, for Warner Bros. (owned by Time Warner (TWX - Get Report) ).

Rowling said she was excited about the artwork for the books: "The artwork and animations in these enhanced editions bring the stories alive in a delightful new way."

The books will be available for $9.99 in 32 countries, starting today. Pre-orders for the French, German and Spanish language versions start on Nov. 9 in 18 additional countries.
