The most-anticipated multiplatform video game this holiday season is Activision Blizzard's (NASDAQ:ATVI) military shooter game "Call of Duty: Black Ops 3," according to a survey by Nielsen (NYSE:NLSN).

"Black Ops 3" is tops on the Nielsen Game Rank study, with a rating of 98%. The latest game in the popular "Call of Duty" franchise is due out Nov. 6 for Microsoft's (NASDAQ:MSFT) Xbox One and Xbox 360 consoles, Sony's (NYSE:SNE) PlayStation 4 and PS3, and Windows PCs.

Nielsen Game Rank measured overall anticipation levels among gamers for games being released in October through December. The research firm polled more than 4,800 gamers in August and September about their awareness, purchase intent and other factors for new games.

Games are given a percent value that reflects how strong the overall anticipation level is for the title, relative to the pre-release anticipation levels of all previous titles that released on the platform or platforms.

"Fallout 4" from Bethesda Softworks and "Assassin's Creed: Syndicate" from Ubisoft tied for second among multiplatform titles with an anticipation rating of 95% each. Racing game "Need for Speed" from Electronic Arts (NASDAQ:EA) came in fourth with 92%. EA's "Star Wars: Battlefront" was fifth with 88%.

Some games are exclusive to individual consoles.

The top exclusive title for the Xbox One is Microsoft's "Halo 5: Guardians" with a rating of 100%, Nielsen said in a report Monday.

Sony's "Uncharted: The Nathan Drake Collection" is the top exclusive for the PS4 this holiday season, with a ranking of 79%.

Nintendo's "Yoshi's Woolly World" is the top exclusive for the Nintendo Wii U console, with a 91% rating, Nielsen said.
