REDMOND, Wash., Oct. 16, 2015 /PRNewswire/ -- Microsoft Corp. will publish fiscal year 2016 first-quarter financial results after the close of the market on Thursday, October 22, 2015 on the Microsoft Investor Relations website at http://www.microsoft.com/investor. A live webcast of the earnings conference call will be made available at 2:30 p.m. Pacific Time.
