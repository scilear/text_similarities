NEW YORK (TheStreet) -- Apple (AAPL - Get Report) shares are falling 0.32% to $113.58 on Friday after customers reported issues while updating their iPhones and iPads with the new iOS 9 operating system, CNBC.com reports.

Some devices crashed as customers tried to install the new system. Users said they lost data that wasn't backed up and others said the updates did not work at all, CNBC.com added.

Separately, the company is moving forward with its self-driving car plans. Apple met with officials yesterday in California to discuss regulations for autonomous vehicles, the Guardian reports.

This action comes on the heels of rivals Google (GOOGL - Get Report) and Uber advancing their plans to create driver-less cars, the Guardian noted.
